Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Plain English: We collect only what we need to run Atlyz. We never sell your data. We never train AI on your customer conversations. You can delete your data any time.

All legal matters, transactions, and payments relating to Atlyz are handled by Iftikhar Ali Shah, who acts as the authorized legal representative for Atlyz. Data-protection requests may also be directed to him under the contact details below.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information — name, email address, and business details when you sign up.
  • Payment information — processed by our payment provider. We never store card details.
  • Business data — website URLs you provide, knowledge files you upload, chatbot settings.
  • Customer conversations — chat messages between your customers and the Atlyz widget.
  • Usage data — how you use the dashboard, feature usage, and error logs.

2. How we use your information

  • Provide and maintain the Atlyz service
  • Power AI chatbot responses for your customers
  • Send you notifications about your account and leads
  • Improve and debug the platform
  • Respond to your support requests

We do not use your customer conversation data to train any AI models.

3. Information sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. We share only with:

  • OpenAI — to power AI responses. OpenAI does not train on API data by default.
  • Infrastructure providers — Railway for hosting. Access is limited to what is necessary.
  • Legal requirements — only if required by law.

4. Data retention

We retain your data while your account is active. On cancellation:

  • Account and conversation data deleted within 30 days
  • Backups purged within 90 days
  • Request immediate deletion: support@atlyz.com

5. Your rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Contact support@atlyz.com to exercise any right. We respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies

We use cookies only to keep you logged in and remember preferences. No advertising or tracking cookies. See our Cookie Policy.

7. Security

We use HTTPS encryption for all data in transit, secure password hashing, and strict access controls. Found a vulnerability? Email support@atlyz.com.

8. Children's privacy

Atlyz is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Contact us to request deletion if applicable.

9. Changes to this policy

We will notify you of significant changes by email or dashboard notice. Continued use after changes means acceptance.

10. Contact us

  • Email: support@atlyz.com
  • Contact form
