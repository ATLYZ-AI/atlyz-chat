Origin
Why we built Atlyz

Small businesses lose customers every day — not because they don't care, but because they can't be everywhere at once. A missed call, an unanswered chat, a question left overnight. Each one is a customer who went elsewhere.

We built Atlyz because the tools to fix this existed — AI, automation, natural language — but nobody had put them together in a way that a small business owner could actually use in five minutes without a developer.

That's the entire mission. AI that works for real businesses, not just tech companies.
