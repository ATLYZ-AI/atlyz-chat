Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2025

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit — like keeping you logged in or remembering your preferences.

Cookies we use

Atlyz uses a minimal number of cookies. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Cookie Purpose Duration Type
session Keeps you logged into the dashboard Session Essential
theme Remembers your dark/light mode preference 1 year Preference
tab Remembers which dashboard tab you were on Session Preference

Third-party cookies

We do not load third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking cookies. If we integrate any third-party tools in the future, we will update this policy and notify users.

Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome — Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox — Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari — Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

Note: disabling the session cookie will log you out of the dashboard and require you to log in again each visit.
