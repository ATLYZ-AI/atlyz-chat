ABOUT ATLYZ
===========
Mission: To give every small business access to the same AI tools that only big companies used to have. AI that works for real businesses, not just tech companies.

Origin Story: Atlyz started with a simple question — why do only big companies get great AI? Small businesses lose customers every day — not because they don't care, but because they can't be everywhere at once. A missed call, an unanswered chat, a question left overnight. Each one is a customer who went elsewhere. Atlyz was built because the tools to fix this existed — AI, automation, natural language — but nobody had put them together in a way that a small business owner could actually use in five minutes without a developer.

Founder / Ownership: Atlyz was founded by a solo developer. The team is small, remote-first, and moves fast. For direct inquiries about ownership or partnership, contact contact@atlyz.com.

Company Values:
- Simplicity first: any business owner can set up in under 5 minutes, no developer needed
- Privacy by default: customer data is owned by the business; Atlyz never trains on or sells conversations
- Speed: response times under 2 seconds
- Inclusivity: 50+ languages supported so any business can serve any customer
- Remote-first, early-stage, high ambition
