DETAILED FAQ — ATLYZ CHAT CAPABILITIES
=======================================

Q: What exactly can Atlyz Chat do?
A: Atlyz Chat can:
- Answer any question a visitor asks, as long as the answer is covered on your website or in your knowledge base
- Respond in the same language the visitor writes in — 50+ languages supported, auto-detected
- Collect visitor contact details (name, email, phone) via a lead capture form when the bot can't fully help
- Notify the business owner by email instantly when a new lead is captured
- Remember the conversation context across the current chat session (last 6 exchanges)
- Embed on any website with a single line of HTML — no developer required
- Be customized with brand colors, greeting message, bot name, bot tagline, and widget position
- Serve unlimited chats simultaneously — no queue, no wait time
- Run 24/7 with under-1-second response time

Q: What can Atlyz Chat NOT do?
A: Atlyz Chat cannot:
- Make phone calls or send SMS — it is a text chatbot only
- Access real-time data such as live inventory levels, order status, or live pricing unless that information is written into the knowledge base
- Book appointments automatically (that is an Atlyz Agent feature, coming later)
- Send follow-up messages after the chat window is closed
- Pull information from password-protected pages, PDFs, or internal databases — it only reads publicly accessible website pages
- Learn or update itself from conversations — knowledge is updated by re-scraping your website or editing the knowledge base manually
- Handle voice or audio input — text only
- Integrate directly with third-party CRMs, calendars, or e-commerce back-ends (that is Atlyz Agent territory)

Q: How does Atlyz Chat handle questions it cannot answer?
A: If a visitor asks something not covered in the knowledge base, the bot politely says it doesn't have that information and offers to connect the visitor with the business owner. It triggers a lead capture form to collect the visitor's name, email, and optionally phone number. The business owner receives an instant email with the visitor's details and the question they asked, so no lead is ever lost.

Q: How accurate is the chatbot?
A: Atlyz Chat answers directly from your knowledge base — it does not guess or hallucinate answers beyond what is written there. If the information is not in the knowledge base, it will say so rather than make something up. Accuracy is directly tied to how complete and up-to-date your knowledge base is.

Q: Can I customise what the bot knows?
A: Yes. After setup, you can re-scrape your website at any time to update the knowledge base. The scraper reads up to 15 pages. You can also contact Atlyz support to add manual knowledge entries for things your website does not cover, such as internal FAQs or pricing details you prefer not to publish publicly.

DETAILED FAQ — ATLYZ AGENT
===========================

Q: What is Atlyz Agent?
A: Atlyz Agent is the third product in the Atlyz lineup, currently in development. Where Atlyz Chat answers questions and Atlyz Voice answers calls, Atlyz Agent goes further — it takes actions. It is a full AI automation layer that connects to your business tools and handles multi-step workflows without any human involvement.

Q: What will Atlyz Agent be able to do?
A: Atlyz Agent is designed to:
- Book appointments automatically by checking calendar availability and confirming slots with customers
- Follow up with leads automatically via chat or email after an initial enquiry
- Handle full order intake workflows — collecting item, quantity, delivery address, and payment details
- Integrate with CRM systems so every customer interaction is logged automatically
- Connect to calendars (Google Calendar, Outlook) to read availability and create bookings
- Run multi-step workflows — for example: customer asks about a service → agent checks availability → agent books the slot → agent sends confirmation → agent adds customer to CRM
- Operate proactively, not just reactively — it can initiate follow-ups, not just wait for incoming messages

Q: How is Atlyz Agent different from Atlyz Chat?
A: Atlyz Chat is reactive and informational — it waits for a visitor to ask a question and answers from your knowledge base. Atlyz Agent is active and operational — it connects to your business tools and completes tasks. Think of Chat as a knowledgeable receptionist and Agent as a full virtual employee who can get things done.

Q: How do I set up Atlyz Chat for my business?
A: The full process takes under 5 minutes:

  Step 2 — Enter your website URL. Atlyz reads up to 15 pages in about 60 seconds and builds a knowledge base automatically
  Step 3 — Customise the widget: set your brand color, greeting message, bot name, and position (bottom-right or bottom-left)

Q: Do I need a developer?
A: No. Pasting the embed code is identical to adding Google Analytics or a Facebook Pixel. If you can paste a script tag, you can deploy Atlyz Chat. No developer, no technical knowledge required.

Q: How long before the chatbot knows my business?

Q: What if my website has a lot of content?

Q: Can I update the chatbot after setup?
