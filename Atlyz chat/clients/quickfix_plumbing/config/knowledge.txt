BUSINESS OVERVIEW
QuickFix Plumbing is a licensed, insured plumbing company serving Austin, Texas and Travis County. Founded in 2012 by Mike Rodriguez. Specialises in residential and commercial plumbing. Known for transparent pricing, same-day response, and a 12-month labour guarantee.

SERVICES
Residential: leak detection and repair, burst pipe repair, faucet and tap install/replace, toilet repair and replace, shower and bathtub repair, water heater install/repair/flush (tank and tankless), drain cleaning and blockage removal, garbage disposal install/repair, sump pump install, re-piping (copper, PEX, CPVC), gas line inspection and minor repair (licensed), outdoor hose bib and irrigation shutoff repair.
Commercial: backflow prevention testing and certification, commercial drain cleaning (restaurants, offices), water pressure balancing, fixture replacement, preventive maintenance contracts for landlords and property managers.
Emergency (24/7 every day including holidays): burst pipes, flooding, sewage backup, no hot water, gas smell assessment (always call 911 and evacuate first for gas leaks).

Can you give a quote over the phone? We give approximate ranges over the phone. A written estimate is always provided on arrival before work starts.
